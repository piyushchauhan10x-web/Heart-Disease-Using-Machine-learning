# Heart-Disease-Using-Machine-learning

Towards Trustworthy Cardiac Diagnosis: An Interpretable Machine Learning Approach Using LIME and SHAP for Heart Disease Prediction
Cardiovascular diseases remain the leading causes of premature mortality worldwide. It requires reliable predictive tools for early-stage detection. Machine learning-based models have demonstrated significant effectiveness in detecting potential risks. However, despite of their high accuracy, their adoption in clinical practice remains limited due to lack of transparency
